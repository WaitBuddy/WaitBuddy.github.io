GALLIPOLI: THE SCALE OF OUR WAR

April 2015 - April 2019

This ground-breaking exhibition tells the story of the Gallipoli campaign in World War I through the eyes and words of eight ordinary New Zealanders who found themselves in extraordinary circumstances.

PETER SNELL: MEDAL-WINNING MAGIC

18 April 2017 - 2 October 2017

Sir Peter Snell was one of the world's greatest athletes in middle-distance running. During his athletic career, he won three Olympic gold medals and two British Empire and Commonwealth Games gold medals, and set at least six world records.
Displayed at Te Papa until 2 October 2017, see two of Sir Peter Snell's Olympic gold medals and one of his running shoes made by his coach, Arthur Lydiard.

THE TE PAHI MEDAL: FORGED IN FRIENDSHIP

19 May 2017 - 19 May 2018

This medal is the first official state gift given to a Māori chief by a foreign leader. Yet this national treasure vanished from Aotearoa New Zealand for over 200 years.
